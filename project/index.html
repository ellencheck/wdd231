About Foreign Language School

Ellen Check Language School offers high-quality language education where all instructors are native speakers. Learn multiple languages: Russian, English, German, French, Italian, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Portuguese.

Language Levels

  • Beginner (A1)
  • Intermediate (B1)
  • Advanced (C1)
  • Proficient (C2)

Our Unique Teaching Method

Students study grammar, vocabulary, and video lessons independently, then practice actively with native speakers. This ensures immediate application of knowledge in real conversation.

Why Choose Ellen Check?

  • Full immersion from the first lesson
  • Practice-focused learning
  • Video lessons and self-study materials
  • All instructors are native speakers
  • Flexible programs for all levels: A1 → C2
  • All languages in one school
