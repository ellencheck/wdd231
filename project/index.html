About Ellen Chek Language School

At Ellen Chek Language School, you can learn multiple languages with the help of **native speakers and experienced teachers**. We offer courses in: Russian, English, German, French, Italian, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, and Portuguese.

Language Levels

  • Beginner (A1)
  • Intermediate (B1)
  • Advanced (C1)
  • Proficient (C2)

For example, Russian courses progress from A1 → B1 → C1 → C2. All other languages follow the same level structure.

Our Teaching Method

Our courses focus on practical learning, not just memorizing vocabulary and grammar. You study grammar, vocabulary, and video lessons independently, then practice with teachers during lessons. This method allows you to apply what you learned immediately in conversation and exercises.

All lessons are taught by native speakers and qualified language instructors, combining correct pronunciation, live communication, and professional teaching methods.

Why Choose Us?

  • Immersive learning from the very first lesson
  • Practice-oriented teaching, not just theory
  • Video lessons and materials for self-study
  • Experienced teachers and native speakers
  • Flexible programs for all levels: A1 → B1 → C1 → C2
  • All languages available in one school
